Blooming Onion

Description

One of my favorite deep fried treats! Nothing beats a blooming onion for dinner. A don't get me started on the blooming onion sauce you make with this dish.

Blooming Onion Ingredients

  • large yellow or white onion
  • all purpose flour
  • milk
  • paprika
  • cayenne pepper
  • garlic powder
  • oregano
  • salt
  • pepper

Dipping Sauce Ingredients

  • mayonnaise
  • creamy horseradish
  • ketchup
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • dried oregano
  • black pepper

Meal Prep Steps

  1. Cut large onion so that it blooms
  2. Put under cold water and help open peddals without breaking them
  3. Mix all purpose flour, paprika,cayenne pepper, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper
  4. Mix eggs and milk in bowl
  5. Dip blooming onion into egg and milk mixture then into the powder mixture
  6. Do step 5 again
  7. Put blooming onion in freezer to harden
  8. Heat vegetable oil in pot
  9. Once boiling dip cold blooming onion into oil for 4 minutes on each side
  10. While cooking, mix all sauce ingredients together
  11. Once blooming onion is golden brown, remove from oil and dry off oil with paper towel
  12. Serve with dipping sauce and enjoy!
