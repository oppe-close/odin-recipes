Mexican Rice

Description

The only way to make rice for a mexican dish. I have not found a better method to get resturant like mexican rice.

Ingredients

  • long grain white rice
  • vegetable oil
  • small onion
  • garlic clove
  • knorr chicken bouillon
  • garlic powder
  • roma tomatoes
  • hot water
  • tsp salt
  • sprigs of cilantro
  • jalapeno

Meal Prep Steps

  1. Fry long grain white rice in pan with vegitable oil until lightly brown
  2. Boil 2 cups of water adding knorr chicken bouillon, garlic powder, roma tomatoes, salt
  3. While the long grain white rice is cooking add choped onion and garlic clove to the mix
  4. Once your water mixture is boiling add rice to it at stur
  5. Stur for a minute and cover with lid
  6. Put on simmer for 45 minutes to an hour
  7. Do Not Open Lid For Anything
  8. After 45 minutes to an hour lift lid and stir with fork
  9. Enjoy!
