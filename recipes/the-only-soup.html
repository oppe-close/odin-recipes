The Only Soup

Description

As the recipe name states this is the only soup I care to know. If I had my own definition of soup it would just be a image of this and what is in it. If it's not this soup, it's not soup.

Ingredients

  • groud beef
  • potatoes
  • canned tomatoes
  • canned corn
  • tomatoe paste

Meal Prep Steps

  1. Fill large pot with water and set to boil
  2. When the water is at a boil, add skinnned potatoes to it
  3. While the water is heating up, cook ground beef in a skillet
  4. Just before the potatoes are soft, add tomaotes, corn, and ground beef to the mix
  5. Let boil for 10 more minutes adding tomatoe paste as needed to thinken soup
  6. Take off heat and let simmer
  7. Enjoy the only soup known to me
